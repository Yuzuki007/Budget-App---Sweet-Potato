🍠 Sweet Potato — Budget

A personal budget tracker that's 100% offline, requires no sign-up, and lives entirely in your browser.

✨ Features

🏠 Dashboard — Get a quick overview of your income, expenses, and remaining balance at a glance
📋 Transactions — Log and manage your income and expense entries with categories, dates, and notes
🎯 Budget Goals — Set monthly spending limits per category and track your progress
📊 Reports — Visualize your spending habits with charts and summaries
💱 Multi-Currency Support — Switch between ₱ Peso, $ Dollar, € Euro, £ Pound, ¥ Yen, ₹ Rupee, and ₩ Won
📤 Export / Import — Back up and restore your data via CSV or JSON files
📲 Installable PWA — Add to your Home Screen on Android or iPhone and use it like a native app
🔒 100% Private — All data stays on your device. No server. No account. No tracking.


🚀 How to Use
Adding a Transaction

Tap the ＋ button at the bottom right
Choose Expense or Income
Fill in the description, amount, date, and category
Tap Save transaction

Setting a Budget

Go to the Budget tab (🎯)
Tap ＋ to set a monthly limit for a category
Track your spending progress with the visual indicators

Viewing Reports

Head to the Reports tab (📊) to see spending breakdowns and trends over time

Exporting Your Data

Go to Settings → Export CSV or Export JSON to download a backup of all your transactions


📲 Install as an App
Android (Chrome)

Open the app in Chrome
Tap the Install banner that appears at the bottom
Or tap the browser menu → "Add to Home Screen"

iPhone / iPad (Safari)

Open the app in Safari
Tap the Share button (⬆)
Choose "Add to Home Screen"
Tap Add

Once installed, Sweet Potato runs like a native app — fully offline!

🛠️ Tech Stack

Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies
Chart.js for data visualizations
LocalStorage for on-device data persistence
PWA-ready with a Web App Manifest and offline support
